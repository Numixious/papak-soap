Ghatre Tala Aryayi company

Ghatre Tala Aryayi company with expert staffs started the career since 1388 in the line of producing herbal oils and handmade soaps from extract of fruits like grapes by Hossein Salemi and after catching the certificates from health and food organization continues the career until 1393 and after 1393 by improving the quality and gathering more devices tried to sign up the company with 7 employeers and keep the work going until 1398 and in 1398 company changed the name to Ghate Tala Aryayi. the other honors of this company is sending handmade soaps and herbal oils to other countries like Japan

Cosmetic soaps with medicinal properties

Herbal soaps have various properties due to their extract, for example, almond oil soap is used to maintain moisture and softness of the skin, or coffee soap is used to exfoliate and eliminate dead skin cells. But in general, the plant properties of soaps can be divided into three general categories:







  • Skin emollient
  • Eliminates dead skin cells
  • Maintains skin moisture and freshness

Cosmetic, medicinal and edible oils

Vegetable oils, like herbal soaps, have a variety of properties, including moisturizing, moisturizing and softening the skin, strengthening the hair, and maintaining the clarity of the hair. Ghare Tala Aryayi Company with a high variety of products in the field of vegetable oils has the ability to offer a variety of products with the following specifications




  • Strengthen and grow hair
  • Disinfectants
  • Repairs and refreshes the skin
  • Skin repairer
  • Anti wrinkle
